About Yorktown Tools

Yorktown Tools was founded in 2025 by a 20-year U.S. Army veteran with a lifelong passion for tools and mechanics. We supply premium name-brand hand tools, power tools, ladders, safety gear, and accessories to commercial contractors and government agencies across Hampton Roads — with free job-site delivery and zero hassle.

Our Promise: You build the project. We deliver the tools — free quotes, free delivery, zero hassle.

Meet the Founder: Zachary Barber

Born and raised in Baltimore, Maryland, Zachary developed an early hands-on passion for tools and mechanics. He trained in automotive and diesel technology and gained industry experience at companies like Ingersoll Rand, AutoZone, R&L Carriers, and a Tools & Accessories firm.

In 2007, he enlisted in the U.S. Army as a turbine engine technician (MOS 15B). Over a 20-year career, he rose to Company First Sergeant and Battalion Operations NCOIC, serving across the U.S., Korea, and Hawaii, with two combat deployments to Afghanistan.

Zachary earned an A.S. in Aeronautics and a B.S. in Project Management from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. His military service instilled discipline, leadership under pressure, and the critical importance of having the right tool for the job — values that now drive every aspect of Yorktown Tools.

Founding this company allows him to support his family, the local community, and fellow contractors (including veterans) with reliable service and premium tools.
