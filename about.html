About Yorktown Tools

Yorktown Tools was founded in 2025 by a 20-year U.S. Army veteran with a lifelong passion for tools and mechanics. We supply premium name-brand tools to commercial contractors and government agencies across Hampton Roads — with free job-site delivery and zero hassle.

Our Promise:
You build the project. We deliver the tools — free quotes, free delivery, zero hassle.

Meet the Founder: Zachary Barber

Born and raised in Baltimore, Zachary developed a passion for tools and mechanics early in life. After training in automotive and diesel technology, he enlisted in the U.S. Army in 2007 as a turbine engine technician. Over 20 years of service, he rose to Company First Sergeant and Battalion Operations NCOIC, with deployments to Afghanistan.

Zachary founded Yorktown Tools to serve fellow contractors and government teams with honest pricing, fast delivery, and real support.
