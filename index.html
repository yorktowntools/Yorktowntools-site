About Yorktown Tools

Founded in 2025 by Zachary Barber, a 20-year U.S. Army veteran and former First Sergeant, Yorktown Tools is a veteran-owned small business registered on SAM.gov. With dual degrees in Aeronautics and Project Management from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, Zachary brings disciplined leadership and technical expertise to every partnership.

We specialize in supplying commercial and government contractors with the exact tools and accessories required to execute flawlessly—on time and on spec.
